## Why

Introduces generator functions as the ergonomic way to implement iterables. After hand-implementing iterators in lessons 03–04, generators show what the boilerplate was hiding.

## What Teaches

`function*`, `yield`, lazy evaluation, `yield*` delegation, `next(value)` for two-way communication.

## Prereqs

04-iterable-protocol

## Success criterion

The test suite in `lessons/05-generator-functions/generators.test.ts` passes.
