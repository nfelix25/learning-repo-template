## Why

Maps the well-known Symbol values to the protocols they unlock. Prereq for iterator/iterable lessons that use `Symbol.iterator` and `Symbol.asyncIterator`.

## What Teaches

`Symbol.iterator`, `Symbol.asyncIterator`, `Symbol.toPrimitive`, `Symbol.hasInstance`, `Symbol.toStringTag` — how each customizes a built-in protocol.
