## Why

Introduces the Symbol primitive — the foundation for every well-known symbol used in the iteration and disposal protocols. Must come first.

## What Teaches

The Symbol primitive: uniqueness semantics, `Symbol()` vs `Symbol.for()`, global registry round-trips, non-enumerability of symbol-keyed properties.

## Prereqs

None.

The test suite in `lessons/01-symbol-primitive/symbol.test.ts` passes.
